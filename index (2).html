introduction to college

The main interest of the Department of Computer Science is to follow up, understand and develop the scientific concepts behind what computers accomplish and how they accomplish it with knowledge of how computers are built and how they work. The department is concerned with analyzing, designing and measuring the degree of complexity of algorithms used in solving real problems. The intelligent human being in the processing of information, and falls within the interest of the Department of Computer Science, teaching and conducting research related to decisions

The main interest of the Information Systems Department is to study all technical issues, senior management issues, and planning policies associated with employing computers in establishing information systems for organizations and institutions.

College Inception

Republican Decree No. 267 of 2006 was issued, which includes the establishment of the Faculty of Computers and Information at Fayoum University. The Supreme Council of Universities agreed, in its session on 9/3/2006, to start studying at the college, starting from the 2006-2007 academic year

Colleage Logo

The logo of the College of Computers and Information consists of two parts. The first part represents the Fayoum driveways, and the second part represents information technology. It consists of two numbers (0, 1) and they represent the transition in information technology from the analog system to the digital.

Vision and Mission

College vision

College of Computers and Information, Fayoum University, aspires to advance and excel in the field of education and scientific research, to achieve a prominent position locally and internationally, and to develop the information society while adhering to professional ethics.

mission

The Faculty of Computers and Information at Fayoum University seeks to prepare specialized and distinguished cadres in the field of computers and information technology, qualified and equipped with theoretical and applied foundations in the field of specialization and capable of continuous learning to compete in the labor market within a framework of ethical values, and to contribute to the development of scientific research and local and international cooperation to serve the community

objectives

.Preparing graduates in computers and information who are qualified with theoretical foundations and application methodologies to qualify them for local, regional and global competition in the development of computer and information technology and its applications.

Providing scientific and technical advice and assistance to bodies and entities that use computer and information technology.

Concluding scientific agreements with corresponding bodies and institutions at the local, regional and global levels in order to exchange opinions and conduct research related to computer and information disciplines.

Degrees

Academic degrees

The university grants upon the request of the college the following academic degrees: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science - Information Systems Duration of study: Four years to obtain a bachelor's degree - The Council of Fayoum University grants upon the request of the Council of the Faculty of Computers and Information a postgraduate diploma in one of the following specializations: - Computer Science (professional) in one of the following specializations: Computer Networks Web Programming - Computer Science (Academy) "Big Data Processing" - Information Systems (professional): "Database Management"
